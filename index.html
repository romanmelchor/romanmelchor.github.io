Purpose

PDX Coders for Costumes gives costumes to kids from all over Portland who are faced with unique challenges, including those with special needs, physical and/or mental disabilities, homeless children, kids living in poverty or at shelters, children with serious medical issues, kids in the foster care system, and kids who have experienced a recent trauma or natural disaster.

Navigate our site to discovers where to get costumes and have a safe Halloween in Portland, Oregon.
